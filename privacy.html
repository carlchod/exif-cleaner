Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Zero Data Collection Promise

EXIF Cleaner Pro is built on a "Zero Trust" architecture. We do not collect, store, or transmit your images or metadata.

2. How the Tool Works (Local Processing)

When you use our tool, all image processing happens locally inside your web browser using JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas APIs. Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server. The EXIF data is stripped directly within your device's memory.

3. Analytics and Tracking

We believe in absolute privacy. We do not use intrusive cookies or third-party trackers like Google Analytics that profile our users.

4. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please reach out to us (see Imprint).
